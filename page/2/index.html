MINING > TAKE ACTION!

Despite the plethora of information, there are so many opportunities to be missed by not acting when you have to act.
Harvard Business School faculty, who have produced many world-class CEOs, point to the recurrence of these unfortunate situations as “lack of information processing capabilities.”
This is because he knows ‘the technology to process information’, but has not learned the ability to process information.
In order to turn data that is merely an array of numbers into meaningful information, we need to know that the last step in information processing is ‘action’.
Businessmen who can smell money like this, once they get information, they act immediately and find a hole for money.
It is wrong to say that you have to get information to get a chance for success.
One step of holding information in your hand will lead you to real success.
